Website Customer Social Network

This is era of social networking. Almost everyone uses social networking websites for different purposes. Social Media helps to connect people from all over the world.

With this module installed, Partners can provide their social media account. Which will be visible in website. By clicking on those links, user will be able to visit partner's social media account page.

This could help seller get proper exposure and can improve its overall sales. From social media, partners can provide information about upcoming events, discounts on products etc. to attract more customers to website.

Just follow simple instructions after installing this module that are mentioned below to reach out to people on social network.

Key Features:

  • Access to all major Social Networking platforms.
  • Easy and One-Time setup.
  • Access for Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, Instagram and Google+.

How to Use


Providing Details

To link social media account, just go to customer page where Social Networks tab is provided. Options for different social media platforms are provided. Just provide the links of account.


In website, a link is provided on the header of website to get details of partner. On Partner page, list of all partners is provided.


Accessing Partner details from website will show all the details of partner. In same page, social media icons are provided. On clicking any of social media icon, it will redirect user to link provided by partner.
